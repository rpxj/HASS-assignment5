So, you want to own a bubble tea shop...

This will be a storytelling piece on the economic growth of the bubble tea market and the difficulties in surviving. It will include:

1. An overview of the Southeast Asian Bubble Tea market – Bubble map to show the size of the market for each country in Southeast Asia or a Lollipop graph or Stacked area graph to show the growth of different markets through time.

2. Breakdown of costs to run a bubble tea shop in Singapore – Simple stacked bar chart.

3. Hotspots in Singapore where competition is the worst – Choropleth map/bubble map to show the parts in Singapore with bubble tea shops; or a Packed bubble chart to show the areas with the most bubble tea shops, versus the ones with fewer bubble tea shops.

The data will come from the study 'Bubble Tea - The business behind Southeast Asia's favourite drink' by Momentum Works and qlub in Aug 2022; as well as Google maps data on bubble tea shops in Singapore.
